TAKE DEBAIT 7.0

“Somewhere between a whispered argument and a stabbing silence lies the truth.”

Something feels off. The lights flicker. The room's too quiet. And that one team? They're too calm.

Welcome to Take DeBait 7.0 — a murderous concoction of debating, deception, and deduction. This isn't your average MUN. This is a courtroom without rules, a game where words cut deeper than blades.

Every round, someone bleeds. Metaphorically (probably). You're not just debating — you're surviving. Your tongue is your dagger, your logic is your alibi. Slip up, and you're the next victim. Or worse... the imposter.
